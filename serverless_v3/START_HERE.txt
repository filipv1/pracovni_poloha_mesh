================================================================================
                      CENY A LIMITY
================================================================================

CENY:
- 10 min video = ~$0.08 (RunPod GPU)
- Storage = $0.015/GB/měsíc (R2)
- Download = ZDARMA! (R2 výhoda)

LIMITY:
- Žádné! Podporuje GB+ videa
- Žádné timeouty (async processing)
- Škáluje automaticky (RunPod workers)
